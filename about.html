How I became a web developer

In around 2021, I decided I wanted to explore the tech industry. I began taking quizzes and researching different jobs. Web Development kept coming up so I began using websites like freecodecamp.org and codecademy. Then I pursued a full-stack web development from Coursera.org. From there I worked my way through a book called Headfirst Javascript. Finally, I decided to take the full jump and go to CareerFoundry where I felt I would solidify my knowledge and skills as well as get the help I need from mentors to reach my goal of having my first web development job.
